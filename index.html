About

I'm a mechanical engineer who took one look at a lifetime of CAD models, panicked, and fled into the world of strategy and startups. No regrets. (Mostly.)

By day-and-night I'm the CEO of mymun, where we help 370,000+ students across 140 countries argue about geopolitics in fluorescent-lit classrooms — lovingly known as Model UN. By the other day, I'm a Senior Associate at Strategy&, currently on educational leave to do an MBA at INSEAD in Singapore.

I've collected degrees and time zones in roughly equal measure — Germany, France, China, Hong Kong, Vienna, now Singapore — and speak five languages with wildly inconsistent confidence. When I get bored, I build little tools that do my chores so I don't have to.
