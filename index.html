About

Currently pursuing an MBA at INSEAD (Class of Dec 2026), while on educational leave from Strategy&, where I work as a Senior Associate. At Strategy&, I've contributed to transformative projects such as developing a €10bn business case for an AI Gigafactory and creating an economic strategy for EXPO Berlin 2035, which gained national media attention. My work focuses on digital transformation, product management, and consulting.

As CEO of mymun, I'm committed to making Model United Nations accessible to students globally. The platform provides comprehensive digital infrastructure for conference organizers and participants, supporting over 370,000 users and 11,000+ events across 140 countries. My leadership has fostered a sustainable business model, ensuring long-term value for the MUN community.
